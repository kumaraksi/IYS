ABOUT US

The India Youth Soccer Association is non-profit making educational Charitable Trust whose mission is to foster the physical, mental and emotional growth and development of India's youth through the sport of soccer at all levels of age and competition. Our job is also to make it fun, and instill in young players a lifelong passion for the sport. The India Youth Soccer Association promotes fair play, and offers leadership in the development of youth soccer in India. At the India Youth Soccer Association, we aim to provide a fun, safe and healthy game for ALL children, rich and poor, boys and girls. Those in Government schools, private schools and the millions who spend part or all of their childhood out of school. Children who want to play for fun, or want to compete at the highest level, who want to play for one season, or who want to play for a lifetime. At the India Youth Soccer Association we recognise that modern soccer for children has been neglected by schools and soccer organisations and that there is a desperate need to give our children the opportunities to play and enjoy that are found in other countries.
